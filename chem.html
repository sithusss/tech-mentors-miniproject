Welcome to Chemical Science Course

The The Bachelor of Science Honours (BSc Hons) degree in Chemical Science is a four-year, programme designed as per the Sri Lanka Quality Framework (SLQF) Level 6, to provide a comprehensive education in chemistry to all our undergraduates.
You can do this as a full time program or part time program.
The main objectives of this degree programme are ,
(1) to bring economic benefits and sustainable development to the country through manufacturing and value addition; and
(2) to contribute to research in chemical science globally.

B.Sc (Hons) in Chemical Science

B.Sc (Hons) in Biological Sciences This programme aims to produce teachers who are competent to teach secondary schools including G.C.E Advanced Level classes. The B.Sc. (Hons) in Biological Sciences specializes in methods to teach subjects of Biology and Chemistry.

Graduate Opportunities

  • The BSc Hons programme aims to provide career and advanced research opportunities in highly demanding disciplines in chemical science. The BSc degree is designed to assist students who aspire to pursue,.
  • Postgraduate studies;
  • Opportunities in the chemical industry;
  • Teaching careers in either private or government based educational institutions.

Entry requirements

Pass in three subjects at the G.C.E Advanced Level examination obtained at three and the same sitting in the Science stream or complete basic course that conducted by institute and a pass in the aptitude test conducted by the Institute.
